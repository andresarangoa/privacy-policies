Account & Data Deletion

ControlBill — Last updated: March 4, 2026

At ControlBill, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can delete your account using any of the following methods:

Option 1: From the App

  1. Open the ControlBill app.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Option 2: By Email

Send an email to controlbillapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your ControlBill account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data Type Action Timeframe
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted Immediately
Profile information (name, photo) Deleted Immediately
Bills and expense records Deleted Within 30 days
Expense groups and shared accounts Deleted Within 30 days
Photos and receipts Deleted Within 30 days
Subscription and payment history Retained for legal compliance Up to 180 days
Anonymous analytics data Retained (non-identifiable) N/A
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your bills, expense groups, and shared account data will be lost. Please export any data you need before requesting deletion.

Additional Retention Periods

Certain data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements:

  • Payment transaction records: retained for up to 180 days as required by financial regulations.
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics: retained indefinitely as this data cannot be used to identify you.
