Privacy Policy

ControlBill — Last updated: March 4, 2026

ControlBill ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help users manage, split, and track shared expenses and bills. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password through Firebase Authentication. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile photo from your Google account.

User-Generated Content: We collect the data you voluntarily enter into the app, including bills, expense groups, shared accounts, person names, and related financial information.

Camera & Photos: The app requests access to your device camera to let you take photos of receipts or bills. Photos are used solely within the app for your records. We do not access your camera without your explicit permission.

Contacts: The app may request access to your contacts to make it easier to add friends or group members. Contact data is used only within the app and is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Device & Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. This includes device type, operating system version, app version, and interaction events. This data helps us improve app stability and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app's features and functionality.
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions via Google Play Billing.
  • To send push notifications (only with your permission).
  • To diagnose technical issues and fix bugs using crash reports.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure). We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase Authentication to protect your information. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud)
  • Google Play Services (Billing, Sign-In)
  • Wompi (Payment processing, Colombia)

5. Notifications

The app may send push notifications related to your bills, groups, or account activity. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

ControlBill is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Revoke camera, contacts, or notification permissions through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
